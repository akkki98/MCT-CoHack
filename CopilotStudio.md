## Build a Training Assistant Agent with MCP

## Scenario

**Contoso Corp**, a leading training service provider, conducts multiple technical training programs delivered by **Microsoft Certified Trainers (MCTs)**. During these sessions, participants frequently ask questions about training topics, labs, certification preparation, and troubleshooting issues.

Currently, trainers respond by:
- Sharing official documentation links
- Typing answers manually in chat
- Searching for relevant content during the session

This process can be **time-consuming**, especially when many participants ask questions simultaneously. Additionally, responses are often not provided in a **structured document format**, which participants could reuse later for revision or reference.

To address this challenge, Contoso wants to build a **Training Assistant Agent using Copilot Studio** that can:
- Answer participant questions automatically
- Retrieve information from training materials
- Generate **structured responses or documents**
- Use **MCP (Model Context Protocol)** to connect with external knowledge sources

---

# Lab Objective

In this lab, participants will build a **Training Assistant Agent in Copilot Studio** that can:

- Answer participant questions during training sessions
